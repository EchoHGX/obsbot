Tap Lock
Users can carry out a variety of shooting modes such as rapid, master and multi-person simply by tapping the desired target in the app.
Power Gesture
Based on visual learning, the gesture control function marks the first time a high-precision and steady gesture control system being integrated into video shooting. Users can even customize 3/5 gestures to trigger certain camera function(s).

Start / Stop

- Tells the camera to start or stop shooting

Select Target / Cancel Target

- Tells the camera to use you as the target or stop using you as the target;

Custom Gesture

- Those three gestures can be assigned with instructions by user itself, one gesture can be assigned with 1 instruction or up to 3 combined instruction string.

technology, OBSBOT Tail ensures stable and smooth tracking and shooting in more complex conditions, such as low light environment, noisy streets, twisted postures, tricky angles, and colorful backgrounds.
A brand new 「 Shar-lock 」 technology can help to regain the lost target quickly even under extreme shooting conditions.
Pin-Pointing
1. OBSBOT Tail simulates the abilities of a professional photographer and automatically adjusts to the perfect composition for every photo and video；
2. You can also define your preferred composition in the app so the device captures you as desired.
Zoom to Fix
OBSBOT Tail gives you the ability to shoot stably and smoothly, with a fixed frame of the target despite the distance (varied with different frames) it is from the camera.
Eureka Moments
OBSBOT Tail automatically captures fun moments of the target(s) into photos and short videos on your phone for you to edit and share seamlessly.
Launch Pad
OBSBOT Tail introduces「launch pad」into video shooting for the first time ever. Tap in app or activate with Power Gesture to trigger over 20 different rhythmed PTZR camera movements and functions like close-ups or full-length scans during shooting.
